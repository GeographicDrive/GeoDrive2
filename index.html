🧱 GP3DT Collision

Rapier3D physics loads from CDN the first time you enable this. Colliders are built live from real GP3DT surface samples every frame (raycast against the actual tileset) — nothing static is placed, so there are no invisible spikes or walls. Works in Car, Bus, and Plane mode.
